    I am Eddie, a Full Stack Web Developer living in Atlanta, GA. I moved here from Central Florida in 2012 to start a children’s photography business with my wife. I received a bachelor’s degree in Physics from Florida State and a doctorate in Entomology from the University of Florida.
    After several years in photography, I decided to pursue a career in which I could combine my artistic strengths from photography with my passion for science, and I promptly enrolled in DigitalCrafts’ four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp in September of 2017.
